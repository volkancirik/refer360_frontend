Onboarding - Finding Waldo! Instructions (Click to Hide and Start the Task)

For this onboarding task you will try to find Waldo’s hiding spot in panoramic images (360 degrees) given a list of instruction sentences written by someone who knows where he’s hiding. This onboarding task is designed to prepare you for the actual HIT tasks. It will give you an idea of how to write clear instructions for finding Waldo. Please read the instructions below carefully. On the right you see three example videos on how to complete this HIT, click on full screen icon on videos to watch them.

  1. Before searching for Waldo, make sure that you are familiar with the whole panoramic image. While looking at the image, click and hold the mouse button while sliding left/right/up/down to change your point of view.
  2. Read the first instruction sentence and follow it by changing your view of the panorama. When you are as close as possible to the point of view the instruction describes, click "Show Another Sentence" to display the next instruction.
  3. After reading and following all instructional sentences, click at a specific location on the image to set your prediction of where Waldo is hiding. A bullseye icon will appear on the image
    1. If you want to remove your prediction, click "Remove Bullseye" or double click on the bullseye icon.
    2. When you are ready, press the "Predict" button to confirm your prediction. This will also reveal the intended location of Waldo.
    3. Rate the difficulty of finding Waldo using radio buttons.
    4. Once you are done click "Next Image".
  4. When you complete the task 4 times you will be redirected to the actual task of hiding Waldo!
  5. If you encounter any problems, please send us email
